Harness your compute

Our Alces Flight service provides you with ability to manage your cloud in a way that best enables you to get your computing done. Whether you want to have your own isolated cluster in the cloud or manage a shared cloud environment Alces Flight can help you achieve your goals.

CONTROL YOUR CLOUD

    • Define a Cloud environment for your own use or share it with others
    • Join an environment that has been shared with you. Once you have joined an environment, you can create a cluster on it to run your computations
    • Create and run clusters on your environments and use them to run your computations. When your computations are completed, shut the cluster down to save on costs
    • Gain access to one of your clusters to launch jobs, keep an eye on their progress and see your results
    • Share one of your environments allowing others to run their computations on it
    • Define the individual components that clusters on your environment can be built from

Simplified Cloud management

Run your computations in the Cloud

With Alces Flight you can construct and launch exactly the HPC cluster you need for your computation. If your needs are varied, we have you covered, Alces Flight will allow you to launch as many clusters as you need, each one tailored to the needs of your computation.

Manage your Cloud environments

Alces Flight provides you with the tools to help your users run their computations on your cloud. Whether you need to provide secured access to your cloud environment; create customized components to meet your users needs; or gain administrator access to solve your users issues, Alces Flight has you covered.
