Effortless deployment

Self-configuring dynamic Galaxy environments

The Alces Flight Galaxy appliance provides researchers and administrators with a simple method to deploy ephemeral or persistent Galaxy compute environments using their existing infrastructure or utilizing available Cloud resource. After launch, the Alces Flight Galaxy appliance configures itself, providing a science-ready environment in just minutes.

Secure Environment

The Alces Flight Galaxy appliance automaticlally generates and configures SSL certificates and public DNS entries, providing you and your fellow researchers a secure platform to work on. The deployed Alces Flight Galaxy appliances also include standard well-known security features, allowing you to focus on your research.
